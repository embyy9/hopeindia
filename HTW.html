How this NGO was formed ?

Hope Own headquarters in Berhampur, Odisha , India located is an NGO . It was established in 2021 and has a presence in many places. As of 2021, the Foundation has reached many families.
Sugyan Barik co-founded this Foundation in 2021 with Adityaraj Mishra and a group of young corporate professionals, primarily works with the blood donations, underprivileged in the areas of education, health, livelihood and empowerment.
